How to use this app
  • 1. Type in a product name or category in the search bar.

  • 2. Choose how many products you want in your .csv file.

  • 3. (Optional) Check for the option to include free shipping information for each product. In the .csv, 'XXX' in the "Free Shipping" column represents 'True', or free shipping is an option for that product; "-" represents False.

  • 4. Click 'Collect Data' to scrape Ali Express for your product information. This process will extract information about each product's name, price, rating, supplier, amount sold, and free shipping (optional); this info will be stored in a .csv file.

  • 5. AFTER YOU SEARCH A PRODUCT: either download the .csv directly on your computer (no account needed), email the .csv to your email address (account needed), or save the data from your latest search in the apps database (account needed).

  • 6. Once you have an account, you can view a maximum of 6 saved queries. You have the option to make a .csv file from your saved query or delete it from the app database.

  • 7. To download a .csv from one of your past queries, you have to click the 'Make CSV' button for that query, then click 'Download CSV'. If you would like to email a csv from a past query, click "Make CSV" then click 'Email CSV'.
